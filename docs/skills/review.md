\# Human Review Skill



\## Purpose



Ensure that all AI-generated reports are reviewed before being finalized.



\## Responsibilities



Display



\- Situation Brief

\- Evidence

\- Map



Allow



\- Approve

\- Needs Review

\- Reject



Record reviewer comments.



\## Inputs



Situation Brief



\## Outputs



Review Status



Approved



Rejected



Needs Review



\## Constraints



Reports cannot be finalized automatically.



\## Success Criteria



Every report has an explicit human decision.



\## Failure Behavior



If no human decision is recorded:



\- Do not save the report.
