\# MVP



\## User



Municipal Emergency Manager



\## Goal



Analyze current weather conditions and known flood-prone areas to identify infrastructure requiring review.



\## Workflow



1\. User clicks "Analyze Conditions".

2\. Retrieve weather forecast from OpenWeather.

3\. Load static flood hazard layer.

4\. Load OpenStreetMap infrastructure.

5\. Perform deterministic spatial analysis.

6\. Generate structured findings.

7\. Generate AI Infrastructure Review Brief.

8\. Human approves or rejects the report.

9\. Save approved report.



\## Success Criteria



\- Live weather data is retrieved.

\- Hazard layer is analyzed.

\- Infrastructure intersections are identified.

\- AI generates a concise review brief.

\- Human approval is required.

\- Approved reports are stored.
